Security Services Services

Based on our own experiences, listening to the needs of our clients, accompanying the business, we develop the services that today can be part of your core processes. Security is not trivial, sensitive user information is our most precious asset.

Security
Assessment

Cyber Security GAP analysis of products. We use agile methodologies and frameworks such as NIST, SABSA, ITIL. We develop the evaluation plan according to the client's needs.

Security
Enforcement

We develop security processes that will add value to development, infrastructure, business and support processes.

Users
Awereness

The end user may be the weakest link in the chain in the processes supported by the business units. That is why a process of awareness is key to give maturity to companies. Phishing, Social Engineering, Malware, best security practices are among others the capabilities that we provide from Valhalla awareness program.

Vulnerability
Management

More than good practices, having a good management of vulnerabilities in the software development life cycle is crucial for our business to be resilient. Our vulnerability management program can greatly reduce the costs of incidents caused by vulnerable code.

Risk
Management

Identifying, Analyzing and Evaluating are only part of the life cycle of our program. The Negotiation of action plans and flexibility with the business are powerful tools to meet our objectives in a safe and scalable way.
